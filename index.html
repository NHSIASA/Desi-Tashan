Northville IASA

Every year, we host Desi Tashan, Michigan's original and largest high school Bollywood dance competition. All proceeds from this event are donated to Vista Maria, a charity that provides aid to abused and trafficked women in Michigan. Over four years, we have proudly donated over $41,000!
